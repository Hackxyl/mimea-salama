      🌱 What is Mimea Salama?

      Mimea Salama is a free AI-powered plant health app designed for East African farmers. It instantly diagnoses plant diseases, pests, and nutrient deficiencies from a simple photo — no expert needed.

      📱 How to Use the App
      1
      Take or Upload a Photo
      Take a clear photo of the affected plant leaf, stem, or fruit in good lighting.
      2
      Tap Analyze My Plant
      Press the green button and wait a few seconds while AI examines your plant.
      3
      Read Your Diagnosis
      Get the disease name, cause, severity, and step-by-step treatment instructions.
      4
      Share via WhatsApp
      Send the diagnosis to family, fellow farmers, or your agro-dealer for help.
      ⚡ Key Features
      🤖 AI Diagnosis 🌍 English & Kiswahili 📋 Scan History 🗺️ Disease Map 🌱 Crop Calendar 🏪 Agro-dealers 📲 WhatsApp Share 🔊 Voice Output 📶 Works Offline 💰 100% Free
      🌾 Supported Crops

      Works on all plants, specializing in East African crops:

      🌽 Maize 🍅 Tomato 🫘 Beans 🍠 Cassava 🍌 Banana 🥬 Kale 🧅 Onion 🌿 & Many More
      💡 Tips for Best Results
      📸 Take photos in good natural light
      🔍 Focus on the most affected area of the plant
      📏 Get close enough to see symptoms clearly
      🚫 Avoid blurry or dark photos for better accuracy
      👤 Create an account to save and track your scans
